About Me

Charles is inquisitive. No topic is too boring and no words are too dull, this includes labels! Charles, or Charlie to everyone, simply can't ignore the rabbit-holes of the web so no topic may go undiscovered ...even those he regrets.

Charlie's past is littered with regrets. These ranging from a history degree, a middle grades teaching job and even a master's in education from NCSU for which he's hoplessly in debt. Today, he can be found studying coding at the UNC-CH Coding Bootcamp where he hopes to finally achieve the American Dream of eating something besides ramen.
